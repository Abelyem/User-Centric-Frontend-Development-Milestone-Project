About us

In early 2017, a group of 5 strangers got together to form what is now The Revolution. Although their backgrounds differed, from the start their goal was to bring people together through their music.

Their music influences have come from great bands such as The Red Hot Chili Peppers, and Linkin Park - the former produced rock, funk, and punk rock music, and the latter usually categorized as alternative rock but throughout their career incorporating various other genres such as pop, hip hop, and electronica.

The Revolution is made up of members who bring their own unique styles to provide music outside of the usually constricted and defined genres.

The group made their entrance to the music industry with their hit record 'Sun' which remained on the The Hot 100 BillBoards chart for a period of 7 weeks. Following this, they released their first album ‘Emerge’ in September 2017.

The album was followed by various singles which contained a mixture of each members’ different style bringing in music lovers from a variety of genres, and always aimed to not be defined by any specific set of rules.

In similar fashion to those they looked up to, whilst having a set style, The Revolution work with, and produce music from different genres (Rock, Hip Hop, Pop - to name a few). This can be seen in their most recent album released only in Novemeber 2019 - 'Prophecy'.

Making great stride, and having had the pleasures of working with the best of the best within the music industry, The Revolution in being lined up for the five-day festival of contemporary performing arts that takes place in Pilton, Somerset (2021) - otherwise known as Glastonbury Festival!
